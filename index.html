Welcome to Moparru Orphanage

Originally founded in 2011,
Moparru orphanage is a place where children can feel safe and happy.
Managed by husband and wife Raju and Lavanya Pallikonda,
the orphanage currently cares for around 30-40 children daily,
making sure they have food, water, clean clothes and a good education.

In recent times, Moparru orphanage has needed more support than ever.
Since the pandemic, sponsorship has decreased massively and
more children are being brought to the orphanage to be looked after.
Please view the gallery to see all the great work that happens at the orphanage.
We really hope you enjoy our website and we would love for you to donate or volunteer with us.
