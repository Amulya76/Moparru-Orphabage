At Moparru, we are always trying to improve things for the children. In recent years, we have had to stop project work and focus solely on providing daily needs for the children because many of our sponsors stopped. However, there are still some projects we would like to continue as this will have a positive impact on our children.

The work for the upper floor of the main building stopped before the pandemic. We would like to provide an upstairs sleeping area for the boys as we have many at Moparru and they need more space since they are growing. This would also allow for a room for the children to relax during the rainy season especially.

If you would like to donate to this project, please click on the donate button below and give what you can. You will be contacted directly by Raju with updates on how your money is positively contributing to this project.
